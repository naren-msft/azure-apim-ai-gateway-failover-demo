# Contoso Electronics — Company Policies

## Remote Work Policy

Contoso Electronics supports a hybrid work model. Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** with manager approval. Fully remote positions are available for select roles. All remote workers must:

- Maintain a secure home office setup with company-approved VPN
- Be available during core hours (10AM–3PM local time)
- Attend in-person team meetings at least once per month
- Complete the annual Remote Work Security Training

## Travel & Expense Policy

- **Domestic travel**: Flights must be booked economy class. Hotel reimbursement up to $200/night.
- **International travel**: Business class permitted for flights over 6 hours. Hotel reimbursement up to $300/night.
- **Meals**: Up to $75/day for domestic travel, $100/day for international travel.
- **Mileage**: Reimbursed at $0.67/mile for personal vehicle use.
- All expenses must be submitted within **14 days** of travel via the Contoso Expense Portal.

## PTO & Leave Policy

- **PTO**: 20 days per year for all full-time employees (accrued monthly). Unused PTO rolls over up to 5 days.
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days per year. A doctor's note is required for absences of 3+ consecutive days.
- **Parental Leave**: 16 weeks paid leave for all new parents.
- **Bereavement**: 5 days for immediate family, 3 days for extended family.
- **Volunteering**: 2 paid volunteer days per year.

## Information Security Policy

All employees must comply with the Contoso Information Security Policy:

- **Data Classification**: Data is classified as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted. Handle each according to its classification.
- **Password Policy**: Minimum 14 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character. Changed every 90 days. No password reuse within 12 cycles.
- **MFA**: Required for all internal systems, email, and VPN access.
- **Device Policy**: Only company-managed devices may access Contoso internal systems. Personal devices may access email only via the approved mobile app with MDM enrollment.
- **Incident Reporting**: Report any suspected security incident to security@contoso.com or call the Security Hotline at ext. 7777 immediately.

## Code of Conduct

Contoso Electronics is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive workplace. All employees are expected to:

- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and professionalism
- Report any harassment, discrimination, or ethical concerns to HR or via the anonymous Ethics Hotline
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts to their manager
- Protect company intellectual property and trade secrets
- Comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies

## Sustainability Commitment

Contoso Electronics is committed to achieving **carbon neutrality by 2030**. Current initiatives include:

- 100% renewable energy in all company-owned facilities
- Zero-waste-to-landfill manufacturing by 2028
- Product take-back and recycling program for all Contoso devices
- Sustainable packaging using 95% recycled materials
- Annual ESG report published on contoso.com/sustainability
