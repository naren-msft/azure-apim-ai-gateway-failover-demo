# Contoso Electronics — Product FAQ

## What products does Contoso Electronics sell?

Contoso Electronics sells a wide range of consumer and enterprise electronics including:

- **Contoso SmartHub Pro** — An AI-powered home automation hub that integrates with 500+ smart devices. Price: $299.99
- **Contoso CloudBook 15** — A lightweight 15" laptop with 16GB RAM, 512GB SSD, and all-day battery life. Price: $899.99
- **Contoso WaveLink Earbuds** — Premium noise-cancelling wireless earbuds with 30-hour battery. Price: $149.99
- **Contoso VisionBoard 4K** — A 32" 4K smart display for video conferencing and creative work. Price: $599.99
- **Contoso PowerCell 20K** — A 20,000 mAh portable charger with USB-C PD 65W fast charging. Price: $49.99

## What is the warranty policy?

All Contoso Electronics products come with a **2-year limited warranty** that covers manufacturing defects. Extended warranty plans (3-year and 5-year) are available at purchase. The warranty does not cover accidental damage, water damage, or unauthorized modifications.

## How do I return a product?

Returns are accepted within **30 days** of purchase with the original receipt. Products must be in their original packaging and in resalable condition. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. For defective products, Contoso offers free return shipping.

## What payment methods are accepted?

Contoso Electronics accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Contoso Gift Cards. Enterprise customers can pay via purchase order (NET-30 terms).

## Does Contoso offer enterprise/bulk pricing?

Yes. Orders of 50+ units qualify for volume discounts (10-25% off MSRP). Enterprise customers also get dedicated account managers, priority support, and custom configuration options. Contact enterprise@contoso.com for a quote.

## What is the SmartHub Pro setup process?

1. Download the Contoso Home app (iOS/Android)
2. Plug in the SmartHub Pro and wait for the LED ring to pulse blue
3. Open the app and tap "Add Device" → "SmartHub Pro"
4. Follow the on-screen Wi-Fi setup wizard
5. Start adding your smart devices through the app's discovery feature

Setup typically takes 5-10 minutes.

## Is there a subscription required?

The SmartHub Pro includes **free basic features** (device control, automation, voice assistant). The **Contoso Premium plan** ($9.99/month) adds advanced AI routines, energy monitoring, remote access, and priority cloud processing.
